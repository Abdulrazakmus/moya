Among The Best Day Trips In Zanzibar

Explore The Islands With Us!.

Prison Island Tour

Prison Island Tour in Zanzibar is a half day private guided tour which consists of snorkeling, visiting giant tortoises, swimming, and discovering of the ancient history the slavery time in Zanzibar.

3 hours
$35 / Person

Stone Town Tour

Stone town tour in Zanzibar is an outstanding private guided walking tour, it takes 3 hours, whereby tourists will get opportunity to discover the historical sites including old slave market, bazaar streets, forodhani garden, spice and fruit market

3 hours
$25 / Person

Dolphin Tour

Dolphin tour in Zanzibar is a half day trip at the fishing village of kizimkazi, south coast of the island, the main attraction in this tour is being able to swim close with the dolphins, for those who are lucky they can even get to play with them,

3 hours
$45 / Person

Blue Safari Trip

Blue safari tour in Zanzibar is a full day trip, this is a sea adventure including swimming, snorkeling, sea foods and sailing within the traditional dhow. The trip starts from 9am and ends by 4pm.

3 hours
$50 / Person

Jozani Forest Tour

Jozani forest tour in Zanzibar is a half day guided tour. This forest holds the rare Red Colobus Monkeys that cannot be seen anywhere else in the world, you may also discover the nature walk and mangroves swamps. visiting giant tortoises, swimming, and discovering of the ancient history the slavery time in Zanzibar.

3 hours
$40 / Person

Mnemba Atoll Trip

Mnemba atoll in Zanzibar Island is full day trip starts from nungwi village. The atoll is just off the northeast coast of Zanzibar island near Matemwe fishing village. Diving and snorkeling at Mnemba is precious, there are schools of colorful tropical fish,

Full Day
$40 / Person

Kuza Cave Trip

the kuza cave is a natural wonder, where the combination of the sandstone rock and mountain river create a beautiful effect. Travel to this magnificent cave has never been so easy. This half day tour is guided by a professional guide, who will explain about the geological formation that took place in the environment, as well as the unique flora and fauna found there.

Full Day
$40 / Person
